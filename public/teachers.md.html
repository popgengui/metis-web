**Teaching notes** [[Back to simulator](index.html)] # Introduction Most models should be straightforward and are not discussed here # Structured models Migration in structured models is given as an absolute value. For example: On an island model with 3 demes and 2 migrants, that means that each deme will export 4 individuals, 2 for each of the remaining demes !!! WARNING The current version does not check if there are enough individuals to migrate, so if you have a deme size of 10, 5 demes and 3 migrants, the code will break. This will be sorted soon. ## Island models Island models do not wrap around. One and two Dimensions are supported. Of course, demes at borders will send and receive less individuals than more cosmopolitan demes at the center. # Recombination Note that the relationship between probability of recombination and genetic distance (d in cM) is: $$ P[recomb] = \frac{1 - e^{\frac{-2d}{100}}}{2} $$ It is **not**: $$ P[recomb] = \frac{d}{100} $$ [[Back to simulator](index.html)]
